## How We Decide What Gets Listed

This site doesn't accept payment, sponsorships, or affiliate commissions —
so when something is listed here, it's because it genuinely cleared the bar,
not because someone paid for placement.

### What we look for

**Reputation & Longevity**  
The service has been operating long enough to have a real track record.
New or pop-up operators aren't listed, regardless of how promising they look.

**Consistent public reviews**  
We look at independent review platforms (Google, TripAdvisor etc.) for
sustained quality — not just a handful of recent ratings.

**Wide availability or significance**  
We prioritize services available across multiple regions of Sri Lanka,
or those that are particularly dominant/well-known in their category.
A niche local operator might be excellent but isn't appropriate for a
general travel guide.

**No reported safety issues**  
Any credible pattern of safety complaints is an automatic disqualification.

**Accessibility & ease of use for tourists**  
Works in English, is reasonably easy for a first-time visitor to use,
and has a functioning support channel.

---

### What automatically disqualifies a listing

- Offers or has offered compensation of any kind for inclusion
- No verifiable track record
- Consistent negative reviews on independent platforms

### Report a listing

No listing is permanent. Services change ownership, quality drops,
apps get abandoned. If you've had a notably bad experience with
something listed here, [let us know](https://tally.so/r/7Ro6O2).

We review listings periodically, but we're a small independent guide —
not a full-time monitoring operation. Use your own judgment and
check recent reviews before booking anything.

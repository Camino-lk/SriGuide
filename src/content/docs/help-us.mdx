This site is a honest, helpful, and freely accessible guide to Sri Lanka. No ads, no paywalls, no sponsored rankings. Just useful information for people who want to explore this island properly.

It takes a surprising amount of time and effort to keep something like this going - researching, writing, fact-checking, building, and maintaining it all while making sure it actually stays useful for real travelers. That's where you come in.

## Ways You Can Help

You don't have to do everything. Even one of these makes a real difference.

### ✍️ Contribute Content

The most valuable thing anyone can offer is first-hand knowledge. If you've traveled Sri Lanka — or you live there — you probably know things that no amount of desk research can replace.

- Spot something outdated or wrong? Flag it.
- Know a hidden gem, a better route, or a local tip worth sharing? Send it in.
- Confident writer? Pages like `Food & Eats` and `Trip Plans` need the most love right now.

<details open>
  <summary>What are these trip plans?</summary>
  Curated itineraries covering key destinations, their locations on a map, and
  practical notes for each stop. They are not tied to any tour operator,
  package, or paid service - just independent suggestions.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I use the recommender?</summary>
  Answer the four questions above and you'll get the plans that best match your
  trip. The closest match sits at the top; the others follow in order of
  relevance. You can start over anytime and try different combinations.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What about accommodation?</summary>
  We don't suggest or promote specific hotels, guesthouses here. Where you stay
  is entirely your call - the plans focus on what to see and do, not where to
  sleep. You can take a look at our [Where to Stay](/where-to-stay) page for
  general info.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Can I change the order of stops?</summary>
  The plans are suggestions, not fixed schedules. Feel free to reorder stops,
  skip places that don't interest you, or spend more time somewhere than the
  plan implies. Treat them as a starting point.
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Are these plans up to date?</summary>
  We review and verify the data manually, but conditions on the ground change.
  Opening hours, entrance fees, road access, and seasonal closures can shift.
  Always double-check anything time-sensitive before you go.
</details>

## 🚨 Emergency Numbers

**Save these to your phone immediately:**

- **Police**: 119
- **Ambulance** (Suwa Seriya): 1990
- **Tourist Police/Tourism Hotline**: 1912 (24/7, English support)
- **Fire & Rescue**: 110

:::tip[Tourist-Specific Help]
**Call 1912** for tourist-related emergencies. Available 24/7 with English support specifically for travelers.
:::

## What to Do in Emergencies

### Accidents

1. Call 1990 (Suwa Seriya ambulance) - FREE service
2. Call 1912 for tourist support
3. Get to nearest private hospital if possible
4. Contact your embassy if serious

### Natural Disasters (Floods, Landslides)

1. Follow local authorities' instructions
2. Move to higher ground if flooding
3. Call 117 (Disaster Management Center)
4. Monitor [Sri Lanka Meteorology Department](https://www.meteo.gov.lk/)

### Theft or Crime

1. Call 119 (Police)
2. Call 1912 (Tourist Police) for English support
3. File a police report (required for insurance claims)
4. Contact embassy if passport stolen

### Lost in Remote Areas

1. Stay calm and don't wander
2. Call 1912 for assistance
3. Use maps.me or Google Maps offline (download maps beforehand)
4. Ask at nearest shop, temple, or guesthouse

## Important Safety Notes

- **Tsunami Warning**: If you feel an earthquake at the coast, move inland immediately. Don't wait for official warnings.
- **Monsoon Season**: Heavy rains can cause flash floods and landslides (May-Sept southwest, Oct-Jan northeast)
- **Wildlife**: Keep distance from wild elephants and monkeys. Call forest rangers if threatened.

## Keeping Safe

- Share your itinerary with someone back home
- Keep copies of passport and insurance in cloud storage
- Have emergency contacts saved offline
- Keep small amount of local cash for emergencies
- Know your accommodation's address in local language
